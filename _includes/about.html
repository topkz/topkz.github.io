About Us


A startup based in Astana, Kazakhstan; our team aims to solve three problems faced by agriculture in Kazakhstan.


1. Billions of dollars are spent on herbicides to fight with weeds.

2. Weeds get resistant to herbicides, due to usage in high doses.

3. Herbicides largely contribute to human health problems and water, soil, and air contamination.
